# Beacon

Beacon is a personal safety app that lets people share their live location, checkpoints, and vital signs with trusted contacts — so someone who cares about them always knows they're okay, and can act fast if they're not.

## Why Beacon

Most people have someone they'd want to know if they were in trouble: a parent, a partner, a roommate. But staying in touch during a commute, a night out, a solo trip, or a run usually means manually texting "I'm okay" or "almost home" — easy to forget, and useless in an actual emergency when you can't reach for your phone.

Beacon closes that gap. It runs quietly in the background and gives trusted contacts visibility and a way to reach you, without requiring constant manual check-ins — and gives you a fast, low-friction way to call for help when you can't type a message.

## Who it's for

- People who want a trusted contact (often a parent) to know they got home safe
- Solo travelers, commuters, and night-shift workers
- Anyone who wants a fast, no-fuss way to alert someone if something goes wrong
- Smartwatch owners who want safety features that don't depend on having their phone in hand

## Core features

**Live location sharing**
Share real-time location with one or more trusted contacts, either continuously during a set window (like a commute) or via a one-tap shareable link that doesn't require the recipient to have the app installed.

**Checkpoints**
Set an expected arrival time or route. If you don't check in by the deadline, or you deviate significantly from the expected path, your trusted contact is notified automatically.

**Panic button**
A fast, low-friction trigger — in-app, via a hardware button, or from a paired smartwatch — that immediately alerts trusted contacts with your location and, where possible, opens a direct line to reach you.

**Heartbeat (smartwatch)**
Passive vitals monitoring via a connected smartwatch. Unusual signals — like a spike in heart rate combined with sudden stillness, or a possible fall or crash — can trigger an alert to trusted contacts without any action needed from you.

**Trusted contacts**
Add the people you want watching out for you, using their phone number or WhatsApp. No app install required on their end to receive alerts or a live location link — though the full experience is richer if they do have Beacon.

## How it works, at a high level

1. You add one or more trusted contacts.
2. You choose how you want to stay visible: a live-tracked trip, a scheduled checkpoint, or just having the panic button and heartbeat monitoring on standby.
3. If something looks wrong — you miss a checkpoint, trigger the panic button, or your vitals suggest a fall or crash — your trusted contact is notified immediately with your location and situation.
4. Your trusted contact can respond directly, whether or not they have Beacon installed.
